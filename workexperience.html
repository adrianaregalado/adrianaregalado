Work Experience

This is a summary of my work experience.

The Ohio State University

Research Assistant, Columbus, OH

During this internship, I interfaced with a hyperspectral imager used for UAV-based data collection, gaining an understanding of the operation of the instrument, procedures for data acquisition, and post-processing. Additionally, I developed MATLAB scripts for post processing and the organization of sensor data retrieved from an environmental data logger (EM60), used for field data collection for environmental monitoring and research. I also created a research website using Drupal to develop unique webpages for the display of publications, research opportunities, research overview, and news or other related highlights.

Claro Enterprise Solutions

Data Analyst Intern, Miramar, FL

As an intern, I developed methods for researching client companies using D&B Hoovers, Bloomberg and other company web pages which allowed for more precise classifications, increasing efficiency. Additionally, I improved the standardization of company headquarter location fields of the entire customer database to allow for faster and more flexible data analysis and report generation. I also created internal instruction manuals that thoroughly explain how to construct 6 different sales operations reports in Business Objects including sales, revenue, and executive dashboard for MNC customers.

Diabetes Research Institute

Research Intern, Miami, FL

This role involved the investigation of the molecular mechanisms regulating pancreatic beta cell function, and required the conduction of immunofluorescence assays, microscopic image acquisition, and data gathering. I analyzed islet cells and exocrine ducts using a laser scanning confocal microscope in order to identify specific locations on cell to maximize cell modification through chemical infusion.
