Foodshed

Coding For Good

Angular, Ionic

Background: The FoodShed organization strives to reduce food waste on campus. They have refrigerators placed in different locations all over campus and stock them with extra food donated by the dining halls and nearby grocery stores. This food is available to UW-Madison students, instructors, and staff.

Task: The Coding for Good club decided to work with FoodShed to create a mobile application that would allow users to find and locate the fridges with ease.
